Mst Rafia Islam

AI Researcher | Human-Centric AI | Legal AI | Computational Social Science

I am Rafia Islam, a fourth-year undergraduate student pursuing Law at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). My research interests lie in exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Human-Centric approaches, and Legal Perspectives. I am actively engaged with the Computational Intelligence and Operations Lab (CIOL) at SUST, working on innovative AI projects.

In addition to my research work, I have strong experience in content design and project management within the Edu-tech sector, having worked with 10 Minute School and Mojaru. My research has been published in CHI'24, and I am working on papers targeting CSCW'24 and EMNLP'24.

Research Interests

  • Human-Centric AI: Developing AI systems that prioritize fairness, accessibility, and user-centric design to align AI technology with societal values.
  • Legal AI: Exploring the intersection of law and AI, focusing on legal research, AI ethics, and the role of AI in legal decision-making and regulation.
  • Computational Social Science: Using AI and computational techniques to analyze human behavior, societal trends, and cultural impacts in digital environments.
