About Me

I am Rafia Islam, a fourth-year undergraduate student pursuing Law at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). My research interests lie in exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Human-Centric approaches, and Legal Perspectives. I am actively engaged with the Computational Intelligence and Operations Lab (CIOL) at SUST, working on innovative AI projects.
